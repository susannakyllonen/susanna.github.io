About Me

Former digital marketer with a newfound passion towards coding

Former Professional digital marketer learning news skills in the domain of frontend development.

Aspiring frontend developer, sports enthusiast and a mother of three inspiring kids. Many would say this combo is hard to manage; I have found that these three important areas of my life give to each other and enable me to be more effective whether I’m working on a new project or prioritizing and making decisions in fast-paced situations.

During my career, I’ve worked in different fields ranging from manufacturing, retail sales & customer service to fashion design. In every position, I have been recognized of my resilience and customer centric attitude. While being at home 2006-2012 when my three kids were small, I studied Marketing & Communication as it was the domain I wanted to work in and develop myself further.

What currently inspires me is to develop myself in the field of software developent. What started a year ago as a spark ignited at the Rails Girls event has now grown into a urge and source of inspiration that keeps me attached to my keyboard and learn new every day.

Those who know me say that I’m warm and empathic towards other people, always dependable and exceedingly interested in learning new things.

But hey, don’t take my words for it, instead reach out to me and let’s see how we can help each other to achieve our goals!
