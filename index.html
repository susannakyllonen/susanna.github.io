About Me

Software developer & founder at Twig the Code

Hard-working software developer, sports enthusiast and a mother of three inspiring kids.

Previously I have been working as a digital marketing professional in different companies ranging from small tech startups to large enterprise companies. Two years ago I felt that the world of marketing had given me all it could and at the same time I was inspired by the technical aspects that I had come across within my work. This introduced me to the world of software development and I fell in love almost instantly. I decided to leave the world of marketing and learn software development.

During my journey to become a software developer, I found out that there are many other women like me who would like to enter the world of software development but due to the male-dominated environment and lack of support communities for women find it hard. I decided to do something for this and founded Twig the Code, a non-profit organization supporting aspiring female software developers on their way.

Twig the code organizes regular coding trainings, development camps, networking events for women and contracts on software development projects to customers. All this aims to help our members strengthen their skills in software development, build up their portfolio through real-life projects and get them familiar with the companies and people behind companies building software in Finland.

Personally, during the two years I have learnt to work with JavaScript, React, Groovy, Python and done projects involving user interface and user experience development.

But hey, don’t take my words for it, instead reach out to me and let’s see how we can help each other to achieve our goals!
